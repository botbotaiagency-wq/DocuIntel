# Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines our data handling practices:

1. **Information Collection:** We collect documents you upload to provide extraction services.
2. **Data Usage:** Your documents are used solely for the purpose of data extraction. They are not used to train AI models.
3. **Data Retention:** Documents are stored securely and are automatically deleted based on your organization's retention policy (default is 7 days). You may also opt for "process-only" mode where documents are deleted immediately after extraction.
4. **Data Security:** All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Access is strictly controlled via Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).
5. **Third-Party Services:** We use third-party AI services for extraction. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and do not use your data for model training.

For inquiries, please contact the administrator.
